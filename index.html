It's Okay to Not Be Okay

2020 | Maturity Rating:U/A 13+ | 1 Season | Romance


An extraordinary road to emotional healing opens up for an antisocial children's book writer and a selfless psych ward caretaker when they cross paths.The series follows Ko Moon-young, an antisocial children's book writer who moves to her hometown to pursue her love interest Moon Gang-tae, a psych ward caretaker, who has dedicated his life for taking care of his autistic older brother Moon Sang-tae.

The series broadcast for 16 episodes on tvN and Netflix from June 20, 2020, to August 9, 2020. It was the most popular show of 2020 in the romance genre on Netflix.The New York Times named It's Okay to Not Be Okay one of "The Best International Shows of 2020".


Starring:Kim Soo-hyun,Seo Yea-ji,Oh Jung-se
Creators:Park Shin-woo,Jo Yong

Twenty Five Twenty One

2022 | Maturity Rating:U/A 13+ | 1 Season | Drama


In a time when dreams seem out of reach, a teen fencer pursues big ambitions and meets a hardworking young man who seeks to rebuild his life."Twenty-Five, Twenty-One" tells a love story between Na Hee-Do (Kim Tae-Ri) and Back Yi-Jin (Nam Joo-Hyuk).The series depicts the romantic lives of five characters spanning from the year of 1998 to 2021.

It premiered on tvN on February 12, 2022, and aired every Saturday and Sunday at 21:10 (KST) for 16 episodes. It is available for streaming on Netflix. The series was a commercial hit and became one of the highest-rated dramas in Korean cable television history.


Starring:Kim Tae-ri,Nam Joo-hyuk,Kim Ji-yeon
Creators:Jung Jee-hyun,Kwon Do-eun

Vincenzo

2021 | Maturity Rating:U/A 16+ | 1 Season | Drama


"Vincenzo" follows the story of Vincenzo Cassano, a skilled lawyer and consigliere for an Italian mafia family. During a visit to his motherland, he finds himself entangled in a battle against a corrupt conglomerate in South Korea, Vincenzo teams up with a determined lawyer to seek justice using unconventional methods, blending dark comedy, crime, and drama. As he navigates the world of law and organized crime, Vincenzo's past and present collide, leading to thrilling confrontations and unexpected alliances.

It aired on tvN from February 20, 2021, to May 2, 2021, every Saturday and Sunday at 21:00 (KST) for 20 episodes; each episode was released on Netflix in South Korea and internationally after its television broadcast.


Starring:Song Joong-ki,Jeon Yeo-been,Ok Taec-yeon
Creators:Kim Hee-won,Park Jae-beom

Descendants of the Sun

2016 | Maturity Rating:U/A 16+ | 1 Season | Drama


"Descendants of the Sun" portrays the love story between Captain Yoo Shi-jin, a skilled Special Forces officer, and Dr. Kang Mo-yeon, a compassionate surgeon. Set against the backdrop of disaster-stricken regions, their romance faces challenges as their demanding professions and personal conflicts intertwine. Opposing philosophies tear them apart, but fate has other plans.Balancing action, romance, and drama, the series explores the sacrifices and choices made by individuals dedicated to saving lives and protecting their country.

It aired on KBS2 from February 24 to April 14, 2016, for 16 episodes.Each episode was released on Netflix in South Korea and internationally after its television broadcast.


Starring:Song Joong-ki,Song Hye-kyo,Jin Goo
Creators:Lee Eung-bok; Baek Sang-hoon

Start-Up

2020 | Maturity Rating:U/A 13+ | 1 Season | Romance


Set in South Korea's fictional Silicon Valley called Sandbox, Start-Up tells the story of people in the world of startup companies.The series revolves around a woman who has dreams of becoming an entrepreneur like Steve Jobs, and her love triangle between a man who is secretly her first love and another man who is pretending to be her first love.This series delves into the challenges and triumphs of creating innovative solutions and pursuing one's passion in the fast-paced tech industry.

It aired on tvN from October 17 to December 6, 2020, every Saturday and Sunday at 21:00 (KST), for 16 episodes. It is available for streaming on Netflix.


Starring:Bae Suzy,Nam Joo-hyuk,Kim Seon-ho
Creators:Oh Choong-hwan,Park Hye-ryun
